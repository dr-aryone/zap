zap!

Here's how simple it is.

All you have to do is copy something you don't understand - or want to learn more about.

zap! will display a notification with some information for you - and you can click on the notification to open details.

zap! will not interfere with a notification if you're trying to copy a big chunk of text - which it can't understand - it will only activate for concise terms.
